What Clauselab does
Five tools, one workspace.
Contracts repository
Track every contract with metadata, status, renewal dates, and tags. Filter, search, and export the whole portfolio as JSON.
Clause library
Sixty-plus practitioner-drafted clauses across confidentiality, indemnification, liability, data protection, and more. Add your own.
Template drafter
Fill a form and watch a draft assemble in real time. Export to Word, markdown, or print. NDA, SaaS, DPA, and consulting templates included.
AI-assisted review (BYOK)
Run issue spotting, redline suggestions, DPDPA and GDPR compliance checks, and plain-English summaries. Bring your own API key.
Portable data
Export a JSON backup in one click. Move your whole workspace across devices without ever touching a server.
Zero-trust by design
No backend, no accounts, no analytics. All storage is in your browser's IndexedDB. AI review uses your own API key, sent directly to the provider.
